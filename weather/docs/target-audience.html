Target Audience

Mybetterweather.com is aiming for an audience of people who are fast paced and looking to find the quickest way to accomplish their tasks. They do not need to be very tech savvy, but our audience will have a routine of looking at some sort of device throughout the day. All ages, but especially those between the ages of 13 and 80.

Scenarios

  • What makes betterweather.com different than other weather sites?
  • Can I get access to this website wherever I may be in the world?
  • How much can I trust Better Weather to give me accurate results?
  • What information does Better Weather give in regards to its weather forecast?
  • With the technology boom, technology is developing quicker than I can keep up. Will I be able to understand this website with my simple technology easily?
